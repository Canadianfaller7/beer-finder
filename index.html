In 1980, there were fewer than 50 brewing companies in the US, and just under 100 brewing locations. Now there are more than 6,000 breweries, of which 99% are small and independent. These breweries provide jobs to their local communities, offer unique flavors of both Old World and new, and bring tourists in from all over.

We at Beer Finder want to help these small businesses thrive by helping get the word out about who they are and where to find them. Have a look at the local flavor options in your community by entering your address below. Your new favorite beer might be just around the corner!
